Omah Cangkem

Studio Omah Cangkem is a cultural space based in Bantul, Yogyakarta, founded by Pardiman Djoyonegoro.

The space has grown into a vibrant hub for artistic exploration, community engagement, and cultural education. Rooted in a deep commitment to preserving Indonesian heritage, Omah Cangkem revitalizes gamelan by making them accessible and engaging for younger generations, inviting people not just to observe tradition, but to live and reinterpret it.
